Staff page to create new events, invite people and handout bicycles.

There exist two different main views you can select. These views present you a summary of the informations currently stored in the database.

The Event View is what you probably need most of the time when handing out bicycles to already registered persons. It shows the people that have been invited and it is possible to handout bicycles.

The Candidate View is to have an overview over all people in the database. It is possible to manually register new persons that do not have an email address or a telefon number. Furthermore, you can take back bicycles from people that have registered long time ago.

The Bicycle View shows all bicycles that have been handed out so far.
